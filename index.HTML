First aid guidlines

  • choking

    • Clutching at the throat/chest.

    Coughing or making attempts to cough .

    Turning red in the face(early on),then agrey/blue colour .

    Look incredibly distrssed .

  • sprains and strains

    • Rest the injured limb.

    Ice the area.

    Compress the area with an elastic wrap or bandage .

    Elevate the injured limb above your heart whenever possible to hlep prevent or limit swelling.

  • liseizur

    • STAY with the person and start timing the seizure remain calm and check for medical ID.

    keep the person SAFE move or guide away from harmful objects.

    Do not put anything in their mouth don't give water,pills or food until the person is awake.

    Do not restrain.

  • head injuries

    • Immobilise the injured person to prevent further injury.

    Secure the neck and head.

    Don't remove headwear and helmets.

    If Unconscioususe the DRSABCD method to perform CPR.
